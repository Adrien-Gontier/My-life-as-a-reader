Who am I

My name is Adrien Gontier. I read for years.

I like detective novels, romances, dramas, thrillers, adventures, ...

I don't stick to any genre.

For writers, I read John Grisham, Dan Brown, Georges Simenon, Stieg Larsson, ...

When I read, I really like to listen to the soundtracks of the films and TV series from which the adaptations are taken. It puts me in a certain mood, a certain atmosphere that I like.

However, I have a code. I never spoil the plots of the works I read. Because I hate when people do that to me.

If I can give you any advice. Only one. For anyone who wants to start reading. It is to begin.
